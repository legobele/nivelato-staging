# Nivelato Support

1. Open a GitHub issue.
2. Wait.
3. The vibes will get to it.

## What "The Vibes Will Get to It" Means

The maintainers will address your issue when the following conditions are met:
- The maintainer (Benj) has sufficient executive function to process the request
- The vibes have been consulted
- The ADHD has temporarily aligned with the task
- The current hyperfixation has released its grip on the maintainer's cognitive resources

This process typically takes 3-5 business days, or 3-5 months, depending on the alignment of the above factors. The maintainers apologize in advance and hope you understand.
