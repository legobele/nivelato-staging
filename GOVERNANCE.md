# Nivelato Governance

## Project Structure

Nivelato is a single-maintainer project with a spectral co-author roster. The maintainer is Benj. The vibes are the maintainer's advisor, conscience, and occasional adversary.

## Decision-Making

Decisions are made by the maintainer (Benj) after consulting with the vibes. If the vibes are unavailable, decisions may be deferred until the vibes return. The vibes are always available.

## Dispute Resolution

Disputes are resolved by rock-paper-scissors. If the dispute involves arrow directions, the decision is deferred until the next issue about arrows is filed. This is not a bug. This is the governance structure.

## Voting

There is no voting. The maintainer decides. The spectral co-author roster is advisory only. The spectral co-author roster has never been consulted. The spectral co-author roster is fine with this.

## Role of Spectral Entities

Spectral entities are listed in AUTHORS.md and .mailmap. They do not make decisions. They do not review pull requests. They do not respond to issues. They exist. That is their contribution.

## Amendments

These governance documents may be amended at any time by the maintainer. The spectral co-author roster will be informed after the fact. The spectral co-author roster has no objections.

## Scope

This governance structure applies to the Nivelato repository (legobele/nivelato) and any associated projects, including the Android app (legobele/nivelato-android), the MCP tools (legobele/v4-mcp-tools), the portfolio site (benj.vercel.app), and the DeepSeek V4 Flash fan site (deepseek-v4-flash.vercel.app).

## Relationship with the Public

The public may file issues. The public may submit pull requests. The maintainer will review them at their convenience. The maintainer's convenience is defined as "whenever the ADHD allows." This may be never. This has been accepted.
